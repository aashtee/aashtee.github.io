Illuminer

Ideas at Work

Where the journey begins...

Our vision is to create products & solutions that can herald a change in how we see, do, learn, experience & live!

Peculiar name for a company with that vision?
Yes it is and chosen very carefully at that because it's the light that guides us home and literally means the following:

Verb French 
(transitive) to illuminate, to light up
(transitive, figurative) to illuminate, to enlighten

Noun English
One who, or that which, illuminates.

Founded in 2020 (Yes that washed out year of Covid lock-down), by some people bored of the rut and wanting to do something to solve some of the day-to-day problems they came across.

The idea was to pick up something that bothered them and go find a solution for it.
Did they succeed all the time?
No! Hell No.
But they are committed to keep on exploring ideas and bringing them to life via some fun start-ups they have and will be co-founding under this banner.
